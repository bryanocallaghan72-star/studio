
# iykyk: Real-Time Cultural Portal

**iykyk** is a local discovery and attribution platform focused on Bondi, connecting the digital "vibe" of social media to real-world venues and experiences.

## App Description
iykyk is a real-time portal to Bondi that connects curated social media stories to local venues. Users discover the neighborhood through "Slice of Life" videos shared by creators, allowing them to browse specific spots and occasionally unlock exclusive local perks. The app’s visual atmosphere and logic dynamically shift through four phases—Dawn, Day, Golden, and Dusk—to ensure recommendations always match the current mood of the street.

## Core Value
It closes the gap between seeing a local spot on social media and actually visiting it by providing direct venue links and claimable rewards. For businesses, it provides a simple way to track which creators are driving real-world foot traffic and redemptions.

## Target Audience
*   **Early Adopters**: Socially active people in Bondi looking for "what’s hot right now," from morning resets to late-night moves.
*   **Lifestyle Creators**: Local influencers who want a dedicated space to share their favorite spots and prove their influence through actual visitor data.
*   **Bondi Venues**: Restaurants, bars, and fitness studios looking to connect with a localized audience through authentic, creator-led recommendations.
*   **Locals & Visitors**: Anyone who wants to skip generic review sites and find the specific spots currently trending in the neighborhood.

## One-Line Summary
A real-time Bondi guide that turns social media recommendations into real-world visits and local perks.
