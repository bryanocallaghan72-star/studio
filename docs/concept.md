BONDI

Turn local vibes into commission.
The referral marketplace for lifestyle creators

**THE PROBLEM**
- Local influencers drive real traffic, but don't get paid unless they sell brand deals
- Micro-creators struggle to monetize local clout
- Local venues want ROI-based promotion, not flat influencer fees
- No bridge exists between these two groups.

**THE SOLUTION**
- A commission-based marketplace where creators promote venues, events, and experiences.
- Earns per lead/conversion via smart links.
- Users discover places through trusted recommendations

**EXAMPLE USE CASES**
- Tina (Bondi girl): "Best brunch spots with outdoor seating"
- Luca (DJ): "Top underground parties this weekend"
- Eva (Fitness content): "Smoothies"

**HOW IT WORKS**
1. Creator apply or join via invite
2. Browse available deals (restaurants, events, parties)
3. Get unique trackable links
4. Post TikToks, Reels, Stories
5. Earn money for every conversion (booking, of, click)
