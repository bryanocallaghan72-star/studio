# **App Name**: iykyk: Real-Time Cultural Portal

## Core Features:

- iykyk Vibe: Mood-based suggestions for various experiences like coffee, sushi, nightlife, and fitness, offering users instant recommendations tailored to their current desires.
- iykyk Flow: Time-of-day rhythm suggestions, adapting recommendations from morning to night to late night, providing users with timely and relevant options throughout their day.
- iykyk My Day: Curated daily itinerary that allows users to shuffle through experiences like a playlist, offering a dynamic and personalized day plan.
- iykyk Fire: Real-time "What’s hot right now" feature with countdowns, FOMO, and urgency to highlight trending cultural moments and time-sensitive offers, incentivizing immediate engagement.
- iykyk Deals: Venue-linked offers, perks, and creator-powered funnels that connect users with exclusive deals and promotions, enhancing their experience with economic incentives.
- iykyk Surprise Me: Shuffle/jackpot style feature that offers playful randomness with the chance of discovering hidden gems and unexpected cultural experiences, adding an element of chance and discovery.
- Community Connector Tool: A generative AI tool to help connect users with themed groups, (e.g., sushi lovers, cocktail hunters), feeling like Discord-meets-IRL, that recommends the top three active groups based on interests.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant coral (#FF7F50) to reflect the energy and vibrancy of Bondi's cultural scene.
- Background color: Soft peach (#F2D7D9) for a warm, inviting feel.
- Accent color: Turquoise (#40E0D0) to highlight interactive elements and draw attention to real-time updates.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a geometric, contemporary feel.
- Use custom, quirky icons that reflect the playful nature of the app. Incorporate location-based imagery specific to Bondi.
- A shuffle deck interface, is clean and modern to focus on the 'now' and reduce clutter. Grid layouts with playful image cards, to show of image.
- Subtle, fluid transitions and animations on card reveals and location changes.
